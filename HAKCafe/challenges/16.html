Challenge 16: Shared Too Much

A recent audit of a small business file server revealed several Windows SMB shares with different permissions. Review the following information and identify the most vulnerable share.

SMB Share Permissions (Host: 192.168.1.15)

  • Share: public
    - Access: Everyone (Read)
    - Description: Shared marketing materials

  • Share: coffee_docs
    - Access: Domain Users (Read/Write)
    - Description: Internal documents for staff

  • Share: projects
    - Access: Engineering Group (Read)
    - Description: Active engineering projects

  • Share: archive
    - Access: Everyone (Full Control)
    - Description: Old backups and misc files

Which share presents the most significant security risk based on its permissions?
